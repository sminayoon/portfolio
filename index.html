Stephanie Yoon

Handbag & Accessory Designer

A fine arts student with an initial interest towards mini bags that led to pursuing a degree in fashion design. She now specializes in fashion product: handbags, SLGs, and footwear.

Being born and raised in Los Angeles, California to a traditional Korean family shaped her personality and taste on design. She uses her background and interests to design sculptural yet feminine collections to tell a story.

BFA in Fashion Design from Parsons School of Design New York, NY.
